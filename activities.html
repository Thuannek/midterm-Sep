reviews

01. Han River Bridge and Dragon Bridge

Watching the Han River Bridge rotate 90 degrees is one of the most interesting experiences in my life, besides, the fact that the Dragon Bridge spits fire and sprays water surprised me because of the outstanding development of Da Nang.

02. Han Market and Con Market

Besides the remarkable development of Danang's buildings, the food stalls and the bustling atmosphere at Han and Con market make me feel like I've been included in a miniature society, very interesting.

03. Local coffee shop

I really enjoy the feeling of sitting at a roadside cafe, sipping a cup of coffee and watching the lives pass in front of me, a very peaceful and relaxing feeling.

04. Local People

The local people here have made Da Nang even more special, they are very friendly and hospitable, ready to help tourists. They are very genuine and honest people
